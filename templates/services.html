PM KUSUM Yojana for Farmers

Vidyut Energy Systems Pvt. Ltd. stands as a prominent Solar EPC Company in Jodhpur, actively participating in the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Urja Suraksha Evam Utthaan Mahabhiyan Yojana (PM KUSUM Yojana).

Overview of PM KUSUM Yojana

The PM KUSUM Scheme is a visionary initiative by the Indian Government to subsidize farmers for installing solar irrigation pumps. Under this scheme, farmers receive a substantial 60% subsidy to set up tube wells and pump sets, along with a 30% loan from the Government.

Objectives of the KUSUM Scheme

We are dedicated to achieving the scheme's objectives, including providing cutting-edge technology to farmers and facilitating de-dieselized irrigation. Our solar pumps enhance eco-friendly irrigation and generate surplus energy, which farmers can sell to the Government to boost their income.

Documents Required for Application

Ensure you have the following documents ready for a successful application:

  • ✔ Aadhar card
  • ✔ Land document (Khasra-Khatauni)
  • ✔ Bank account passbook
  • ✔ Declaration form
  • ✔ Mobile number
  • ✔ Passport size photo

Benefits of the KUSUM Scheme

Our solar EPC company actively supports the KUSUM Scheme, providing the following benefits:

  • Financial Support: Subsidies and loans covering up to 90% of the total installation cost.
  • Income Generation: Boost your income by selling excess power back to the Government.
  • Land Utilization: Use barren land to generate a stable income for over 25 years.
  • Eco-Friendly Farming: Mitigate pollution on farms with clean, renewable energy for irrigation.

Application Process

Following a successful application, farmers must deposit 10% of the total cost to the supplier designated by Vidyut Energy Systems. The solar pump set will be operational after the subsidy amount is sanctioned, a process typically taking 90 to 100 days.
